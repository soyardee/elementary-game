HOW TO PLAY:
The goal is to get as many points as possible by shooting asteroids and stars.
However, you need to answer questions in order to reload your ship's weapon.

To collect a star, you must shoot it and then it becomes available to pick up for extra points.
Asteroids are worth 1 point, stars are worth 3.

CONTROLS:
Side arrow keys to move left and right
Up arrow fires a shot.
Down arrow reloads.
